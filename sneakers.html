AllChinaBuy sneaker finds and shoes spreadsheet links

A focused checklist for searchers comparing AllChinaBuy shoes spreadsheet links, sneaker QC photos, insole measurements, seller batches, and international shipping risk.

Start with the real shoe listing

AllChinaBuy sneaker finds are useful only when the spreadsheet link still points to a live product page. Before ordering, check the current colorway, size range, seller photos, material close-ups, stock status, and whether the item title matches the shoe you intended to buy.

Check sneaker QC photos before shipping

Warehouse QC matters more than the spreadsheet row. Compare toe box shape, heel height, sole thickness, stitching, color accuracy, detail placement, lace quality, and glue marks. For shoes, request insole measurement photos when sizing is uncertain.

Use measurements, not size labels

AllChinaBuy shoes spreadsheet notes may say true to size or size up, but the safer check is insole length. Compare the seller size chart and warehouse measurement with a pair you already own, especially for EU, US, UK, and China size conversions.

Plan shipping weight and packaging

Sneakers can change haul cost quickly because boxes add volume and weight. Decide whether the shoe box is worth keeping, then compare the estimated package weight with your shipping route before paying for international shipping.
