Platform research

What AllChinaBuy actually does, and how to use spreadsheets around it

Updated July 1, 2026 - Independent research note

AllChinaBuy presents itself as a Taobao agent and China shopping service. Its public homepage says users can browse products from Chinese shopping platforms including Taobao, 1688, and Tmall, then use AllChinaBuy for purchase support, quality inspection, photography, warehouse storage, and international delivery.

That makes a spreadsheet useful as a discovery layer, not as proof that an item is safe to buy. A spreadsheet row can help you find a product idea quickly, but the buyer still needs to open the live listing, confirm current stock, review size charts, and wait for warehouse QC photos before committing to the final parcel.

Marketplace scope

AllChinaBuy says it helps users shop from Chinese platforms such as taobao.com, 1688.com, and tmall.com.

Agent workflow

The homepage describes a flow of order and pay, quality check and storage, then international logistics and parcel delivery.

Warehouse checks

AllChinaBuy states that purchase, quality inspection, photography, and 90-day free storage are part of its service pitch.

Support tools

The official navigation includes a shipping calculator, parcel tracking, shipping solution inquiry, customized logistics, and DIY orders.

Where spreadsheets help

Spreadsheet-style lists are best for narrowing search intent: sneakers, hoodies, jackets, pants, bags, accessories, seller notes, and rough price expectations. They save time when browsing broad marketplaces, but they should be treated as leads. Prices, photos, colors, seller inventory, and product pages can change after a row is shared.

What to verify before shipping

  • Open the current product page and confirm the item still matches the spreadsheet description.
  • Compare seller photos with warehouse QC photos before international shipping.
  • Use measurement photos for shoes, pants, jackets, and oversized tops where fit risk is high.
  • Estimate shipping with weight, package volume, shoe boxes, and route restrictions in mind.
  • Keep source links, seller notes, size choices, and QC decisions in one place before submitting the parcel.

Payment and service context

AllChinaBuy's public homepage lists payment-related options such as PayPal, international credit card, telegraphic transfer, Visa, Mastercard, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Alipay, and top-up support. Availability can vary by account, region, and current policy, so payment and shipping decisions should always be checked inside the live AllChinaBuy account before checkout.
