AllChinaBuy spreadsheet categories for streetwear and fashion finds

Start with the product type, then check listing photos, size chart notes, seller details, QC risk, and shipping weight before adding an item to an agent haul.

AllChinaBuy sneaker finds and shoes spreadsheet links

Sneaker rows should be checked for shape, sole thickness, material photos, insole measurements, batch notes, and color accuracy. A useful shoes spreadsheet entry points you toward the listing, but the warehouse QC photos decide whether the pair is worth shipping.

AllChinaBuy hoodie finds, sweatshirts, and fleece links

For hoodie finds, look beyond the front graphic. Check fabric weight, sleeve length, shoulder drop, zipper quality, wash effect, and whether the seller provides real measurements. Asian sizing can run small, so compare the size chart with a hoodie you already wear.

Browse Hoodie Finds

AllChinaBuy jacket finds and outerwear spreadsheet links

Jackets are higher-risk spreadsheet items because weight, lining, zippers, embroidery, and seasonal sizing all matter. Before shipping a shell jacket, bomber, puffer, or workwear coat, request close-up QC photos and check the estimated package weight.

Browse Jacket Finds

AllChinaBuy pants finds, cargos, denim, and shorts

Pants spreadsheet links are most useful when they include waist, inseam, thigh, rise, and leg opening notes. Wide denim, cargos, sweatpants, and shorts can look right in product photos but fail on fit, so measurement photos are more important than the item title.

Browse Pants Finds

AllChinaBuy bag finds, accessories, and jersey links

Accessory finds should be checked for hardware, stitching, strap length, logo placement, and whether the listing photos show the actual item. Bags, jerseys, hats, belts, and small add-ons are easy to add to a haul, but low-quality accessories can make an otherwise clean order feel cheap.
