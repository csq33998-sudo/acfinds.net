AllChinaBuy spreadsheet link updates and corrections

Spreadsheet links can change quickly. This page explains what should be reviewed when a product disappears, a seller changes a batch, or a category needs a clearer browsing path.

When a spreadsheet link needs review

A link should be reviewed when the product is out of stock, the seller changes photos, the price jumps sharply, size options disappear, or QC reports show a different item than the listing advertised.

What a useful correction includes

Good corrections include the product category, the old link, the issue found, and the safer browsing direction. For example: sneaker sizing changed, hoodie measurements are missing, jacket weight is too high, or pants photos no longer match the spreadsheet note.
