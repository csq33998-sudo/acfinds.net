Warehouse QC

AllChinaBuy QC photos guide for spreadsheet finds

Use this checklist to verify AllChinaBuy warehouse QC photos, product measurements, seller accuracy, stains, stitching, sizing, and return decisions before shipping a haul.

Compare QC photos with the listing

Open the original product listing next to the warehouse QC photos. Check whether the color, material, print placement, logo position, shape, and included accessories match what the seller advertised.

Look for visible defects

Common AllChinaBuy QC issues include stains, loose threads, crooked embroidery, misaligned prints, damaged zippers, uneven soles, missing tags, and wrong color variants. Catch these problems before international shipping, when returns are easier.

Request measurement photos when fit matters

For hoodies, jackets, pants, and shoes, ask for measurement photos if the fit is important. Compare chest, shoulder, sleeve, waist, inseam, length, leg opening, and insole length with items you already own.

Decide whether to keep, exchange, or return

A spreadsheet find is only a lead. If QC photos show a wrong item, major defect, bad sizing, or seller bait-and-switch, fix it before paying for shipping. A smaller confirmed haul is usually better than shipping uncertain items.
