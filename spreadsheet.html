What belongs in a useful AllChinaBuy spreadsheet?

  • Clear categories for sneakers, hoodies, outerwear, tees, pants, bags, jerseys, and accessories.
  • QC photo reminders for shape, stitching, print alignment, color, labels, and size measurements.
  • Notes for parcel weight, seasonal use, outfit matching, replacement risk, and seller reliability.
  • A store path only when the buyer is ready to verify the live listing and build an agent order.

QC checklist

Warehouse photos should answer specific questions before the item ships internationally.

Sneakers

Check toe shape, heel tabs, sole glue, logo scale, box labels, lace quality, and insole measurements.

Hoodies and tees

Compare chest width, print centering, wash effect, cuff shape, neck label, and fabric weight.

Bags and accessories

Review hardware color, zipper movement, strap stitching, detail placement, and inside labels.
