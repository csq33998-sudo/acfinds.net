AllChinaBuy spreadsheet vs random agent links

An organized spreadsheet should help you find products faster, understand risk sooner, and avoid wasting warehouse fees on links that were never checked properly.

What makes a spreadsheet useful

A useful AllChinaBuy spreadsheet is not just a pile of URLs. It groups products by category, uses clear item names, points to live listings, and gives the buyer enough context to check sizing, QC photos, seller reliability, and shipping weight.

Why random links waste time

Random social links often lack product context. You may not know if the item is still in stock, whether the seller changed the batch, or whether the photos match the version people originally shared. That makes verification slower and increases return risk.

When curated browsing is better

Curated browsing works best when you already know the category you want: sneakers, hoodies, pants, jackets, bags, or accessories. Starting from a category keeps the search focused and makes it easier to compare similar finds before ordering.
